Custom Highlight

A chrome extension for customizing highlight color.
Highlight text the way that you want it. Easy, beautiful, personal. Change Highlight background and text-color. Uses hex color codes for easy copy and pasting and includes auto color for the text that gives the most contrast.

For feature-requests and bug-reports visit the Github-page or contact me via email. Help is much appreciated.

This extension uses pickr library by Simonwep.
