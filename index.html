Custom Highlight

A chrome extension for customizing highlight color.
Change Highlight background and text-color. Uses hex color codes for easy copy and pasting and includes auto color for the text that gives the most contrast. Works on pretty much all websites, including Google Docs. Compatible with Dark Reader.

For feature-requests and bug-reports visit the Github-page or contact me via email. Help is much appreciated.

This extension uses pickr library by Simonwep.
